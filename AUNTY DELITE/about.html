Our Story

Bringing authentic flavors into a modern, welcoming space.

Aunty Delite started as a passion project to bridge the gap between traditional African culinary richness and the relaxing atmosphere of a modern cafe. For years, we've poured our hearts into perfecting recipes passed down through generations, ensuring every dish brings a taste of home.

We are a family-owned business dedicated to quality. Whether you are grabbing a quick pastry on your way to work, sitting down for a restorative bowl of jollof rice, or trusting us to cater your most important celebrations, you become part of the Aunty Delite family.
