Our Story

Bringing authentic flavours into a modern, welcoming space.

Aunty Delite started as a passion project to bridge the gap between traditional African culinary richness and the relaxing atmosphere of a modern café. For years, we have poured our hearts into perfecting recipes passed down through generations, ensuring every dish brings a taste of home.

We are a family-owned business dedicated to quality above all else. Whether you are grabbing a freshly baked pastry on your way to work, sitting down for a restorative bowl of jollof rice, or entrusting us to cater your most important celebrations — you become part of the Aunty Delite family the moment you walk through our doors.

Every plate that leaves our kitchen carries with it the warmth of a mother's care, the pride of a proud heritage, and the promise of a memory worth keeping.
